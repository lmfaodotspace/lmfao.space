Open Secret Paris

xD SPACE is hosting Open Secret Paris at La Générale on May 30, 2025. Doors open at 19:00, screening at 19:30. Open Secret is a touring internet cinema and screening series that explores the porous boundaries between digital culture, artistic collaboration, and experimental film.

Launched in April 2024, it resists fixed definitions, instead allowing each screening to recontextualize the work and reshape its meaning. Rooted in a network of artists with strong online practices, Open Secret emphasizes the ephemeral, memetic, and infrastructural nature of internet based films that evolve, glitch, reformulate, and speak to the conditions of their circulation. More than a program, it's an ongoing inquiry into how moving images move through us, online and off.

Open Secret Paris is composed of works by @carcrashdaddy, @dansdansrev, @redactedcut, @johnrobinbold, @eliskajah, @xafyalovecraft, @poorspigga, @marble.index, @babygorggotustoned, @mischaapje.

110 minutes. No subtitles.
